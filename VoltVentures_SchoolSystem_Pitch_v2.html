The Current Reality

Most schools are still running on paper, Excel, and guesswork.

01
Report cards done manually

Teachers hand in mark sheets, someone types them up, errors happen, reprinting costs money, and term end is a crisis every time.

02
Fee tracking is a mess

Bursars use exercise books or Excel. Outstanding balances are unclear. Parents dispute payments. The head never knows the real picture.

03
Staff records are scattered

Contracts in a drawer, attendance in a book, payroll in someone's head. HR at most schools is completely undigitised.

04
No real-time visibility

The headteacher has to ask four different people to get a picture of what is happening. There is no single source of truth.

05
Internet is unreliable

Most systems stop working when internet goes out. Bursars cannot record payments. Everything halts. That is not acceptable here.

06
Generic systems don't fit

Every school has their own report card format, grading scale, and fee structure. One size never fits all and nobody builds for Uganda.

Who Uses the System

Each person sees only what they need.

Administrator / Principal

The head has full visibility into everything — finance, academics, HR, inventory — from one dashboard. They manage, they do not do the data entry.

Desktop + Web Access
  • Live dashboard: fees, results, staff, inventory at a glance
  • Weekly auto-generated financial and academic summary
  • Full school configuration and grading scale settings
  • User management: accounts, roles, passwords, permissions
  • Broadcast announcements to parents and students
  • Pull any report across any module at any time
  • Override or approve actions system-wide
  • Audit log of everything every user has done
Finance / Bursar

The bursar's entire workflow lives in the system. Fee collection, receipts, outstanding balances, expenses, and financial reports — all offline-capable.

Desktop — Offline Capable
  • Separate fee structures for day scholars and boarders
  • One-click term fee allocation to all students
  • Record cash, MTN, Airtel, and bank payments
  • Print official numbered PDF receipts immediately
  • Real-time outstanding balances by class or student
  • Apply scholarships and waivers with mandatory reason
  • Record and categorise all school expenses
  • Daily, term, and annual financial reports
Director of Studies

The DOS enters marks, runs the grading engine, and generates report cards for the whole school — in the school's own format — without a spreadsheet.

Desktop — Offline Capable
  • Mark entry per subject per assessment type
  • Automatic grade and O-level division computation
  • Generate report cards using your school's custom template
  • Bulk generate an entire class's report cards as PDFs
  • Lock marks after deadline to prevent late edits
  • Class performance summaries and subject pass rates
  • Manage end of year promotions and retention
  • Build and print exam timetables
HR / Administration

HR manages the full staff lifecycle — from onboarding to monthly payslips — without a spreadsheet or a drawer full of paper.

Desktop — Offline Capable
  • Full staff profiles with documents and contracts
  • Daily staff attendance with monthly reports
  • Leave applications, approvals, and balance tracking
  • Payroll with NSSF and PAYE deductions computed
  • PDF payslip per staff member per month
  • Disciplinary and performance records with timestamps
Parents

Parents check their child's results and fee balance from home without calling the school. Works on any smartphone over the web.

Web Portal — Smartphone Friendly
  • View child's report card after school publishes it
  • See outstanding fee balance and full payment history
  • Download payment receipts as PDF
  • Receive WhatsApp alerts for fee reminders and new results
  • View school announcements and calendar events
  • Update contact information directly
Students

Students access their academic record from home. Results, report cards, fee statements — all from their phone or computer.

Web Portal — Read Only
  • View results per term after DOS publishes them
  • Download report card as PDF
  • Full academic history across all years enrolled
